# Physics — Beginner Curriculum Outline

A first-ever-physics course for complete beginners (see `docs/content-standard.md`). No calculus
assumed; ideas first, light algebra only, everything defined from scratch. Each module below becomes
a sub-topic; each generates 3-4 lessons with readings + diagrams + questions, tiered easy→hard.

### Module 1: What Physics Is — Units & Measurement
- Concepts: what physics studies (matter, energy, motion, forces) and the idea of models; the
  scientific method (observe → hypothesize → predict → test); physical quantities & units; the SI base
  units (meter, kilogram, second, ampere, kelvin, mole, candela); scientific notation & powers of ten;
  orders of magnitude & estimation ("Fermi" guesses); unit conversion & dimensional analysis; precision
  vs accuracy, significant figures; reading measuring tools.

### Module 2: Motion in a Straight Line (Kinematics)
- Concepts: position, distance vs displacement, speed vs velocity, acceleration; reading motion graphs
  (position-time, velocity-time); the constant-acceleration equations; free fall and g.

### Module 3: Vectors & Motion in Two Dimensions
- Concepts: scalars vs vectors; arrows, components, adding vectors; 2-D velocity/acceleration; projectile
  motion (independent x and y); intro to circular motion (centripetal idea).

### Module 4: Forces & Newton's Laws
- Concepts: force as a push/pull; Newton's 1st (inertia), 2nd (F = ma), 3rd (action-reaction); mass vs
  weight; free-body diagrams; friction, tension, normal force, gravity near Earth.

### Module 5: Energy, Work & Power
- Concepts: work = force × distance; kinetic & potential energy; conservation of energy; power = energy/time;
  efficiency; everyday energy examples.

### Module 6: Momentum & Collisions
- Concepts: momentum = mass × velocity; impulse; conservation of momentum; elastic vs inelastic collisions;
  why airbags/crumple zones work.

### Module 7: Rotation & Torque
- Concepts: angular position/velocity/acceleration; torque (twisting force); moment of inertia (idea);
  rotational vs linear analogues; angular momentum & why skaters spin faster.

### Module 8: Gravity & the Solar System
- Concepts: universal gravitation (idea + inverse-square); orbits as "falling around"; weight on other
  worlds; tides; satellites.

### Module 9: Fluids
- Concepts: density & pressure; pressure with depth; buoyancy & Archimedes; Pascal's principle (hydraulics);
  flow & a gentle Bernoulli intuition.

### Module 10: Oscillations & Waves
- Concepts: oscillation, period, frequency, amplitude; simple harmonic motion (pendulum, spring);
  transverse vs longitudinal waves; wavelength/frequency/speed; reflection, interference, resonance.

### Module 11: Sound
- Concepts: sound as a pressure wave; pitch & loudness; speed of sound; the Doppler effect; beats & standing
  waves in instruments.

### Module 12: Heat & Temperature (Thermodynamics intro)
- Concepts: temperature vs heat; thermal energy; conduction/convection/radiation; specific heat; phase
  changes; gas laws (idea); the laws of thermodynamics in plain language; entropy intuition.

### Module 13: Electricity & Magnetism (intro)
- Concepts: electric charge; current, voltage, resistance & Ohm's law; simple circuits (series/parallel);
  magnets & fields; electromagnetism (current makes magnetism); how motors/generators work (idea).

### Module 14: Light & Optics
- Concepts: light as a wave & ray; reflection & mirrors; refraction & lenses; the electromagnetic spectrum;
  color; how eyes/cameras/glasses work.

### Module 15: A Peek at Modern Physics
- Concepts: atoms & their parts; the idea of relativity (time/space aren't absolute); quantum weirdness
  (energy in packets, probability); radioactivity; what's still unknown. Inspirational, conceptual.
